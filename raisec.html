RAISEC TEST

RESULTS OF THE RIASEC TEST

R

R = Realistic

These people are often good at mechanical or athletic jobs. Good college majors for Realistic people are…

  • Agriculture
  • Health Assistant
  • Computers
  • Mechanic
  • Engineering
  • Food and Hospitality

Related Pathways

  • Health Services
  • Business
  • Public and Human Services
  • Industrial and Engineering Technology

I

I = Investigative

These people like to watch, learn, analyze and solve problems. Good college majors for Investigative people are

  • Marine Biology
  • Engineering
  • Chemistry
  • Zoology
  • Medicine/Surgery
  • Consumer Economics
  • Psychology

Related Pathways

  • Health Services
  • Business
  • Public and Human Services
  • Industrial and Engineering Technology

A

A = Artistic

These people like to work in unstructured situations where they can use their creativity. Good majors for Artistic people are...

  • Communications
  • Cosmetology
  • Fine and Performing Arts
  • Photography
  • Radio and TV
  • Interior Design

Related Pathways

  • Public and Human Services
  • Arts and Communication

S

  • Education

Related Pathways

  • Health Services
